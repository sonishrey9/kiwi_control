# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Kiwi Control to be a respectful, practical, and inclusive open-source project. Contributors, maintainers, and users should be able to collaborate without harassment, intimidation, or hostility.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and direct
- assume good intent while still giving honest technical feedback
- focus critique on code, design, and behavior, not people
- help keep discussions concrete and actionable
- respect project boundaries and safety constraints

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- doxxing, intimidation, or threats
- repeated bad-faith derailment of issues, reviews, or discussions
- pressuring maintainers to ignore safety or trust boundaries

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or ban participants for behavior that violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you need to report a conduct issue, open a private security/contact route described in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) or contact the maintainers directly through the project’s listed support channel.
